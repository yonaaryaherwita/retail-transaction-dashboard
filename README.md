# Retail Transaction Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive retail transaction dashboard built in Looker Studio to monitor business performance and support data-driven decision making.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer activity, product categories, payment methods, and transaction trends. It is designed to help stakeholders quickly identify business opportunities and monitor key performance indicators (KPIs).

---

## Business Objective

Retail businesses generate large amounts of transaction data every day. Without proper visualization, it can be difficult to identify sales trends, customer behavior patterns, and top-performing products.

This dashboard was developed to transform raw transaction data into meaningful insights that can support operational and strategic decisions.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Executive KPI Summary

Monitor key business metrics at a glance:

- Revenue
- Profit
- Customer Count
- Quantity Sold
- Discount Amount

### Sales Trend Analysis

Track sales performance over time to identify growth patterns, seasonality, and revenue fluctuations.

### Product Category Analysis

Analyze the contribution of each product category to overall sales performance and transaction volume.

### Payment Method Analysis

Evaluate customer payment preferences and understand the distribution of transactions across different payment methods.

### Product Performance

Identify top-performing products based on revenue, quantity sold, profit, and discount value.

### Customer Analysis

Explore customer-level performance and identify high-value customers based on transaction activity and revenue contribution.

### Interactive Filters

Allow users to dynamically explore data through available dashboard filters.
